Loan Calculator

Loans can be complicated.

This tool aims to give you the knowledge to quickly, and visually, see how changing a little can change a lot. What does a loan look like at 5.0% interest vs 5.25%? What if you borrowed £5,000 less, or added another £100 to the monthly repayment?

Try out the Loan Calculator →

For those that are interested: This is written in Vue.js, using version 3's Composition API, and Chart.js.
